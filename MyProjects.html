App Description

This app is designed to create a school emergency plan during an unexpected or a situation where the students/ pupils and the staff are in danger from external threats. This app is encrypted for everybody inhabiting the school as it requires a DUSD username and password. After filling out all of the important details, then the app will track the person who is using this app. Then the floor plan of Dublin High School will appear and it will try to show all of the possible exits for the student. Furthermore, as the person tries to escape, they will get a notification if they want to report this situation or the usage to the authorities. The app will then immediately call the emergency line (911) and there will be an immediate response (sound effects) quoting that they have tracked their location and that they will immediately arrive at their destination. The app in another screen will show the estimated ETA for the officials to arrive. The starting location will be the Dublin Police Headquarters right by Alameda County library.
